Anshoo Tandon



Hi, I am Anshoo Tandon, currently the Director of Research at the IUDX Program Unit, SID, IISc. I have extensive research experience in the broad area of information and coding theory, having worked closely with Vincent Tan, Teng Joon Lim, and Mehul Motani. I received my Ph.D. degree from the National University of Singapore, the M.E. degree from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India, and the B.E. degree from Kumaun University, Nainital, India.

My past industry experience spans over 9 years, where I worked in lead/senior roles with telecom semiconductor companies such as Broadcom, ARM, Freescale Semiconductor (merged with NXP), and Analog Devices, on design and implementation of efficient and optimized transceiver algorithms for cellular and wireless connectivity solutions.

My current research focus is on differential privacy, privacy preserving computing, and statistical signal processing. See my publication list for details.
